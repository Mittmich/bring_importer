Pasta Carbonara

🍽 4 servings ⏱ 30 minutes
Ingredients
  • 400g spaghetti
  • 200g guanciale
  • 4 large egg yolks
  • 100g Pecorino Romano
  • Freshly ground black pepper
  • Coarse salt
Instructions
  1. 1Bring a large pot of well-salted water to a boil. Cook spaghetti until al dente.
  2. 2Cut guanciale into cubes and fry over medium heat until crisp and golden. Remove from heat.
  3. 3Whisk egg yolks with grated Pecorino Romano and a generous amount of black pepper.
  4. 4Reserve ½ cup pasta water, then drain. Add drained pasta to the pan with guanciale.
  5. 5Off the heat, pour in egg mixture. Toss rapidly, adding pasta water a splash at a time until glossy.
  6. 6Serve immediately with extra Pecorino and black pepper.
