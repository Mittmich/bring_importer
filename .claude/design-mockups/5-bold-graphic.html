Pasta
Carbonara

🍽 4 servings · ⏱ 30 minutes
Ingredients
400gSpaghetti
200gGuanciale
4Large egg yolks
100gPecorino Romano, grated
—Freshly ground black pepper
—Coarse salt, for pasta water
Instructions
  1. 01Bring a large pot of well-salted water to a boil. Cook spaghetti until al dente, then reserve ½ cup pasta water.
  2. 02Cut guanciale into cubes and fry over medium heat until crisp and golden. Remove from heat.
  3. 03Whisk egg yolks with grated Pecorino Romano and a generous amount of black pepper in a bowl.
  4. 04Drain pasta and add to the pan with guanciale. Toss to coat in the fat.
  5. 05Off the heat, pour in egg mixture. Toss rapidly, adding pasta water a splash at a time until glossy and creamy.
  6. 06Serve immediately with extra Pecorino and a generous grind of black pepper.
