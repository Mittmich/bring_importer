🍝 Pasta Carbonara

🍽 4 servings ⏱ 30 min
Ingredients
400g spaghetti 200g guanciale 4 egg yolks 100g Pecorino Romano Black pepper Coarse salt
Instructions
1Bring a large pot of well-salted water to a boil. Cook spaghetti until al dente.
2Cut guanciale into cubes and fry over medium heat until crisp and golden. Remove from heat.
3Whisk egg yolks with grated Pecorino Romano and a generous amount of black pepper.
4Reserve ½ cup pasta water, then drain. Add drained pasta to the pan with guanciale.
5Off the heat, pour in egg mixture. Toss rapidly, adding pasta water a splash at a time until glossy.
6Serve immediately with extra Pecorino and black pepper.
