Pasta Carbonara

🍽 4 servings ⏱ 30 min
Ingredients
400g spaghetti
200g guanciale
4 large egg yolks
100g Pecorino Romano
Freshly ground black pepper
Coarse salt
Instructions
01Bring a large pot of well-salted water to a boil. Cook spaghetti until al dente.
02Cut guanciale into cubes and fry over medium heat until crisp and golden. Remove from heat.
03Whisk egg yolks with grated Pecorino Romano and a generous amount of black pepper.
04Reserve ½ cup pasta water, then drain pasta and add to the pan with guanciale.
05Off the heat, pour in the egg mixture. Toss rapidly, adding pasta water until glossy.
06Serve immediately with extra Pecorino and black pepper.
