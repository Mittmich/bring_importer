Pasta Carbonara

🍽 4 servings ⏱ 30 min
Ingredients
400g spaghetti
200g guanciale
4 large egg yolks
100g Pecorino Romano
Freshly ground black pepper
Coarse salt
Instructions
01 Bring a large pot of well-salted water to a boil. Cook spaghetti until al dente.
02 Cut guanciale into cubes and fry over medium heat until crisp and golden. Remove from heat.
03 Whisk egg yolks with grated Pecorino Romano and a generous amount of black pepper.
04 Reserve ½ cup pasta water, then drain. Add drained pasta to the pan with guanciale.
05 Off the heat, pour in egg mixture. Toss rapidly, adding pasta water a splash at a time until glossy.
06 Serve immediately with extra Pecorino and black pepper.
